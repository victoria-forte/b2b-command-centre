Commercial Questions

Commercial

General

  • Company overview: Industry? Products? Customers? Brand?
  • How much do you know about Shopify?
  • What is your compelling event to act?
  • Why Shopify B2B?
  • What is your current set up for B2B?
  • How are B2B orders originating today?
  • What are the main business challenges?
  • What are the main functional/technical challenges?
  • What is your desired set up for B2B?
  • Please detail all the B2B requirements.
Commercial

Revenue

  • What is the global revenue of the company?
  • What is your total B2C revenue (online and offline)?
  • Online B2C revenue?
  • What is your total B2B revenue (online and offline)?
  • B2B Revenue split between B2B portal, Inbound and manual sales, EDI, Punchout, marketplaces (other streams)?
  • Of that revenue, how much will originate online and go through Shopify? (B2B revenue online + projected B2B revenue online)
  • How many B2B orders annually?
  • B2B AOV?
Commercial

Payments

  • How will your B2B customers pay for their orders?
  • Will you allow your B2B customers to pay via credit/debit card if they wish?

Functional & Business Questions

Functional

B2B Buying Process

  • Walk me through how a current B2B customer places an online order with you?
  • Why are you looking to replace this process?
  • Do you sell both D2C and B2B from the same storefront? Or do you have a completely separate B2B online store?
  • Are B2B buyers required to login to a B2B portal to place an order?
  • If yes, when they login do they see products and prices specific to their company profile? Or do all wholesale customers see the same prices and products?
  • How do new B2B customers request access or request to become a B2B buyer with you now? What does that process look like?
Functional

Structure of Price Lists, B2B Catalogs & Pricing Assignment

  • Tell me about your B2B pricing structure.
  • If more than one pricing tier or fixed price, how do you determine the pricing tiers or fixed prices a given customer receives?
  • Do you have different price tiers for different segments of customers?
  • If yes, can you elaborate on these audiences and how they are assigned?
  • How many price tiers do you have and how are they managed?
  • What types of products do you sell? Are they customizable/personalized/digital/perishable, etc.?
  • If yes, how is this currently accomplished/managed?
  • Do you sell products in minimums, multiples?
  • Are your minimums and multiples at the variant or product level?
  • How often do your prices change?
  • Do you offer any additional discounts on top of your wholesale prices based on the amount of product a customer orders or current promotions you're running?
  • If yes, please describe the type of promotions you run (volume pricing, promotional discounts, e.g. 5% orders over $2,000)?
  • Who are your customers? Where are they located?
  • What terminology do you use when referring to your customers? (e.g. Partners, Dealers, Vendors, etc.) If multiple terms, explain the differences.
  • Are there any other unique customer attributes we should be aware of?
  • What would you like a customer to be able to do or change on their own in terms of self-serving their own orders and accounts?
  • Is it ever the case that a buyer's manager or a senior team member needs to approve an order after it's placed by the buyer?
  • How do your buyers search/add products to an order? (re-order, order from a list, import a CSV, search for SKU/part numbers?)
  • Do you have any minimum or maximum order restrictions?
  • How many line items are on an average order?
  • Do your customers often create recurring purchases or quick re-orders?
  • Do you offer pre-orders or seasonal selling?
  • Do you need to capture Purchase Order numbers or other information at time of checkout?
  • Do you accept quotes before orders?
  • If yes, please describe how this process works.
  • Do you need to manually approve orders?
  • Do any of your orders come through EDI?
  • If yes, what percentage and how is this currently managed (system integrations, etc.)?
  • Are you looking for Shopify to manage any part of this process?
  • If a B2B customer doesn't order with you online, how will they order with you?
Functional

Sales Reps / Customer Service Reps Placing Orders on Behalf of Customers

  • Are your sales reps solely employed by your company (staff reps) or are they independent?
  • What information should your sales reps have access to? Should they be able to see all customers, orders, and price lists?
  • Can sales reps modify pricing or add promotions ad-hoc?
  • Will sales reps have access to wifi when meeting with customers?
  • Aside from order-taking, what responsibilities does your sales rep have when meeting with customers (e.g. troubleshooting, new customer training)?
  • How do you process payments for B2B orders? Would your customers be paying for/processing orders in Shopify or is this done externally?
  • Do you offer payment terms?
  • If yes, what percentage of B2B buyers are on terms?
  • What are all the forms of payments you are looking to accept? (CC, ACH, check, etc.)
  • Do you offer customers store credit?
  • Are there spending limits for your customers per day/month/etc.?
  • If yes, what are the limits and how are they managing this with their current customers?
  • Do you have any use cases where customers are charged tax?
  • Are there any other complex tax requirements that need to be taken into consideration?
  • Do you include shipping at checkout for B2B orders?
  • If not, when are shipping charges applied and how are they calculated?
Functional

Shipping & Delivery

Note: It's common for B2B orders not to include a specific shipping cost at checkout. In this case, an admin would add shipping to the order once confirmed.
  • Do you have any custom shipping types or requirements?
  • If yes, how is this currently managed?
  • Do you provide pickup options for customers?
  • Are there special/custom pickup instructions customers need to be aware of?
  • Do you have any partial / delayed shipment scenarios?
  • When orders are placed, will they originate in an ERP and feed into Shopify? Will they originate in Shopify and need to feed into other systems (like an ERP)?
  • Please elaborate on the rest of your current tech stack.
  • Are there any other unique workflows related to discounts, pricing, shipping, customers, orders, etc. that are important to your current workflow?
  • Any additional unique personnel or logistical considerations that need to be accounted for?

B2B Discovery Framework

Framework

Minimum Viable Discovery — Order Origination Channels

For each channel, determine: Does the merchant use it? and What is the estimated revenue ($)?

  • Does this merchant have an existing self-serve B2B buying experience?
  • Does this merchant have sales representatives or manually input orders?
  • Does the merchant have any portion of their business selling to big box stores (Walmart, Nordstrom, CVS, etc.)? Alternatively, if tech savvy — do they have any part of their business using EDI?
  • Do the merchant's buyers use any e-procurement software, or punchout to place orders? (Punchout = connecting to the merchant's online store and placing orders directly from the buyer's e-procurement software)
  • Does the merchant have a retail location where buyers can place an order and pay via POS?
  • Does the merchant sell via a marketplace (e.g. Faire)?
Framework

Will Shopify Be Source of Truth? — B2B Primitives

Company, Location & Customer Management

  • Is the prospect currently using a CRM or ERP to manage companies, locations or customers?
  • Map the merchant's company, locations and customers hierarchy.
  • Does the prospect offer payment terms to their companies?
  • How and when will new customers, locations and companies be created?
  • Do the prospect's buyers have credit limits or buying limits? Describe how these limits work.
  • Does the prospect have any use cases where customers are charged sales tax? Describe the scenario.

B2B Buyer Pricing

  • Does the prospect currently use an ERP or PIM to manage buyer pricing?
  • How often are prices refreshed? (At time of checkout, once per minute, daily, never?)
  • Describe how the merchant offers volume-based discounted pricing to their B2B customers.
Framework

B2B Product Management

  • Does the prospect currently use an ERP or PIM to manage their products and which products are available to each B2B buyer?
  • What kind of products is the prospect selling? Physical? Digital? Subscriptions? Sold by unit? Or weight and dimension?
  • Do you sell products in minimums, multiples?
  • Does the prospect sell products that require custom configuring, pricing and quoting (CPQ)?
Framework

Day-to-Day Operations on Shopify

These questions guide how we position Shopify to solve their day-to-day management of the business.

  • What kind of payments are accepted by the prospect? Roughly what percentage of orders fall in each payment type? (Credit card, bank payment, cheque/cash, wire transfer)
  • What's your highest number of line items on an order?
  • Do you offer pre-orders, seasonal selling or subscriptions?
  • Are you selling both B2B and DTC? Blended vs dedicated?
  • Do the prospect's employees or sales reps require special permissions? Are they assigned to specific accounts? Or is their commission based on individual orders?
  • Do the prospect's employees or sales reps place orders on behalf of customers via the online store (i.e. impersonate them)?
  • What functionality are they looking for in their customer accounts?
  • Does the merchant want buyers to be able to manage their own locations and buyer profiles & permissions?
  • Do the merchant's buyers require approval workflows (e.g. buyer one creates the cart, buyer two approves and places the order), or do multiple buyers collaborate on the same order?
  • Does the prospect's customer leverage an existing account setup to check balances and pay open invoices?
